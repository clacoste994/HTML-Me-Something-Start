Types of Zombies

  1. Classic

    Some people make the mistake of considering these zombies a walk in the part. Get it? Its because they can't run and only shamble around. Don't let your guard down too though. You can find yourself surround my hundreds of these decomposing fleshbags before you know it.

  2. Mutated

    Now these andrenaline pumped freaks are not your grandma's zombies. The run, they climb, and they won't stop until they'll ripped your throat out! Dob't expect to come out unscathed after an encounter with these monsters. Even the prepared will have a hard time seeing the next day while fighting these everyday.

You and The Zombie

Some would say the most dangerous aspect of the zombie threat is the zombie's former humanity. I tend to view that thinking as hogwash. They are dead and we are living! Even if the zombie facing me was a family member when they were living, I'd blast them away without a second thought! Thinking and wasting time with empathy will lead you on the fast track to getting devoured!

Avoid The Zombie

  1. Going North

    This is one of the more popular idea. Zombies lack body heat, so they will simply freeze in the cold. Thats simple, but the cold isn't very forgiving to humans either. There will be so many people heading up north that they will lack both the resources and infrastructure to shelter all the refugees.

  2. Going South

    This is obviously the preferred option. Yes, zombies can survive in the heat, but for how long? The heat will decompase they fast and they shouldn't be a threat past a season or two. There's also the add plus that most people will head north, leaving more resources for the wise few that go south into the heat.
